Art Petrosyan

Art Petrosyan is an engaging visual storyteller with experience conceptualizing and executing short video projects from beginning to end. He has more than 10 years experience shooting and editing promotional and educational videos and designing various publications for non-profit organizations. His skills include green screen work, lighting, studio shooting, location shooting, and multiple post-production responsibilities.

One of his most memorable projects was interviewing Liza Minnelli for a short tribute film about French chanson entertainer Charles Aznavour.

A native of Armenia, Art is a graduate of the Gevorkian Theological Seminary. He unexpectedly discovered a passion for visual storytelling in his 20s and changed course, going on to study cinematography at the School of Visual Arts and graphic design at Hunter College. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Communication Arts from North Park University and a master’s in Digital Imaging and Design from New York University. Art is fluent in Armenian and Russian.
